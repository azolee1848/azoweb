Landing page

In digital marketing, a landing page is a standalone web page, created specifically for a marketing or advertising campaign. Unlike web pages, which typically have many goals and encourage exploration, landing pages are designed a single goal, known as a call to action. It's this focus that makes landing pages the best option for increasing the conversion rates of your marketing campaigns and lowering your cost of acquiring a lead or sale. - www.unbounce.com

Personal webpage

Personal web pages are created by an individual to contain content of a personal nature rather than content pertaining to a company. Personal web pages are primarily used for informative or entertainment purposes but can also be used for personal career marketing by containing a list of the individual's skills, experience and a CV. - wikipedia.org

Corporate website

A corporate site presents a company to all its audiences and not only to its customers or users. The corporate site is classically distinguished from the commercial site intended only for customers or users. On the content side, the corporate site endeavors to promote the company, its history, values, commitments, purpose, etc. and not directly its products or solutions. - wearecom.fr
